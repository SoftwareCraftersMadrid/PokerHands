Poker hands kata

The kata

http://codingdojo.org/cgi-bin/index.pl?KataPokerHands
Your job is to compare several pairs of poker hands

The cards

  • A poker deck contains 52 cards
  • Each card has a suit which is one of clubs (C), diamonds (D), hearts (H), or spades (S)
  • Each card also has a value: 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, 10 (T), jack (J), queen (Q), king (K), ace (A)
  • The suits are unordered
  • The values are ordered with 2 being the lowest and ace the highest

Hands

  • High Card
  • Pair
  • Two Pairs
  • Three of a Kind
  • Straight
  • Flush
  • Full House
  • Four of a kind
  • Straight flush

Sample


Black: 2H 3D 5S 9C KD  White: 2C 3H 4S 8C AH
Black: 2H 4S 4C 2D 4H  White: 2S 8S AS QS 3S
Black: 2H 3D 5S 9C KD  White: 2C 3H 4S 8C KH
Black: 2H 3D 5S 9C KD  White: 2D 3H 5C 9S KH

Output


White wins. - with high card: Ace
White wins. - with flush
Black wins. - with high card: 9
Tie.
